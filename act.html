Earthquake Preparedness

How to stay safe during and after an earthquake

Before an Earthquake

  • Secure heavy furniture to walls
  • Create an emergency plan with family
  • Prepare an emergency kit with supplies
  • Identify safe spots in each room

During an Earthquake

  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On
  • Stay away from windows and heavy objects
  • If indoors, stay indoors
  • If outdoors, move to an open area

After an Earthquake

  • Check for injuries and provide assistance
  • Inspect utilities for damage
  • Listen to official information
  • Be prepared for aftershocks
